📊 About USPS

The United States Postal Service (USPS) is an independent agency of the executive branch of the U.S. federal government responsible for providing postal service in the U.S. Founded in 1971, it serves over 160 million delivery locations across the country.

🚚 Service Types

First-Class Mail: Letters and small packages. Priority Mail: 1-3 day delivery. Priority Mail Express: Overnight to 2-day delivery. Media Mail: Books and educational materials at lower cost.

💰 Pricing

USPS offers competitive rates for mail and parcels. Stamps start at $0.68 for First-Class Mail. Flat-rate boxes available for predictable shipping costs. Free shipping supplies (boxes, labels, tape) available online.

⏱️ Delivery Times

First-Class: 1-3 business days. Priority: 1-3 days. Priority Express: Next day/2 days. Times vary by destination. Track your package anytime online.

🌟 Did You Know?

USPS is the only delivery service required by law to reach every address in the U.S., including remote areas and territories. They deliver to over 160 million addresses, 6 days a week, rain or shine!
